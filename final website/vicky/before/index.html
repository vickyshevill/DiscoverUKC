Before you come to University

➤

The hope for this page is to give you some useful advice on what to do before coming to UKC. Before I give you that advice, I want to say that most likely you are nervous and worried about moving away from home, possible for the first time. I want to tell you that ok and normal. Everyone is scared when they come to university or any big change happens. I know I was and the best advice I got was remember that everyone is in the same boat as you so go make friends and have fun. And don’t worry about making a fool of yourself. You won’t!

OK, so now I will give you some actual tips of things you should do before coming to Canterbury. First, you should get your accommodation all sort out, either living on campus or off. In my opinion, in the first year, you should live on campus as its less stressful and easier to go to events on campus. Once you have the accommodation sorted you should join the freshers' Facebook page to find your housemates so you can get to know them before you arrive at UKC. Next thing to think about is your finances, you don’t want to spend all your money in the first week so you should make a weekly budget. Remember to include extra money for going out, especially for freshers’ week.

Before coming to Canterbury, you may want to decide if you would like to get a uni rider bus pass, this allows you to take any bus in Canterbury and some parts of Kent for a year. So, if you are planning to take a bus to town a lot for shopping or nights out. You want to get a bus pass however they are £100 plus for the year so think about it carefully. So, I hope this advice helps you when you start your UKC story and when you get here check out are During University page. For more tips and place you should go when you are in Canterbury.
