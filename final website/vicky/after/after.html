Last Weeks At University

➤

So, you are in your final weeks at UKC and trying to get everything sort before you get that graduation gown on. There so much to do in such a small time, you are worried about what to do with all the stuff you bought over the year when you were in Canterbury and trying to find that perfect job. So, here are some helpful tips and place you should check out to help you close this chapter on your life.

The Kent union does this amazing initiative where you can donate food to the foodbank to support other students who need a little helping hand. There is also an Upcycle scheme which allows you to donate pretty much anything from bedding to pots and pans. If you donâ€™t know what upcycling it is is basically taking other student donations and instead of them going into landfills, they are given away for free to students who really need them. These donations can be dropped off during the many collection and donation events throughout the year. Just check the student union for more information.

So now that you have pack up and got rid of all the unwanted stuff you had, its time to have some fun with your friends and to find that perfect dress or suit for graduation. Wait, you are still worried about getting a job. The university has a great network to help you find a job after your degree finishes, they offer free career support for three years after your graduation. The amazing people at the careers and employability service will help you find job opportunity through the alumni network which is set up.

Hopefully, these pages have helped you to make the most out of your UKC life and put you on the path to your future. Just from everyone here, I would like to say good luck and you will do amazing!
