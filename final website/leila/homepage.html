Welcome to Discover UKC!

This is a website designed specifically for students attending the University of Kent to help them throughout all stages of uni from beginning to end. Check out the different pages for tips and hacks on surviving uni life: we have advice on everything from food, friends, and fun!

For International Students

Being an international student can often make the university expereince confusing and difficult. However, by getting to know your uni and the area you'll be living in, you can make the transition easier. Learn about Canterbury and connect online with future friends and housemates!
